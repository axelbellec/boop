About

I needed a way to secure my bike, but I couldn't find any bike racks.

So, I built a website that maps out bike rack locations in Bordeaux.

It pulls data from the Bordeaux Metropole Open Data platform to show various rack types, including ones for motorbikes, standard bikes, and cargo bikes.

Click on a rack to see how many hoops are available.
